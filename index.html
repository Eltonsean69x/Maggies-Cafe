Visit Us

Maggie's Cafe is located in the heart of Ornskoldsvik. Cozy seating, lots of natural light, and a warm place for dogs and owners to meet. We keep water bowls, towels, and a small play corner for well-behaved pups.

About Maggie

Maggie is a friendly Bully-Mix who inspired this space. She loves naps on top of me and meeting new friends. The café supports local shelters — ask about our adopt-a-pup days.

House rules
  • All dogs must be supervised and on a short leash indoors.
  • Please ensure vaccinations are up-to-date.
  • Clean up after your dog — bags provided at the counter.
